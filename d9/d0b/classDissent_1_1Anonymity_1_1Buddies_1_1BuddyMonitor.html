Detailed Description

a 1-to-1 mapping between Nyms and members.

This monitor implements a version of the "Hang with Your Buddies to Resist Intersection Attacks;" however, it lacks the features that allow for uniformly randomly assigned nyms and continuation of nyms across rounds. Calculations in this version take into consideration the 1-to-1 mapping and certain metrics have meaning only within this context.
